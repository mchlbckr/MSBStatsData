# Environmental survey sample (EU, n = 10)

The dataset contains responses of 10 sampled persons from an EU-wide
survey on environmental protection.

## Usage

``` r
env_survey_eu10
```

## Format

A tibble with 10 rows and 4 variables:

- age:

  Age of respondent.

- gender:

  Gender of respondent.

- income_eur:

  Monthly net income in EUR.

- environmental_protection:

  Attitude towards environmental protection.
