# Company profile sample

Sample of four companies used in introductory tasks on variables and
scales.

## Usage

``` r
company_financials
```

## Format

A tibble with 4 rows and 5 variables:

- company:

  Company name.

- employees:

  Number of employees.

- annual_revenue_mio_eur:

  Annual revenue in million EUR.

- equity_share_pct:

  Equity share in percent.

- credit_rating:

  Rating category.
